About Us

Who are we?

We are a service designed to finding you the perfect place stay and host events here in Central Florida. Whether you are looking for a place to host your bachelor/ette party, looking for the right place for your spring break trip or even just looking to get away for the week, we’re here to meet your needs.

Often times these events are a call to celebrate and sometimes those celebrations become a bit wild. We get it, plus cleanup sucks, so included in our services we do provide house cleaning services for after the party is over. Now this doesn’t mean to go wild and destroy everything, you are still liable for any damages you may cause but it does mean if there is a mess, we’ll take care of it for you.
